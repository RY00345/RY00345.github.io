Regarding NingDeYuan

NingDeYuanFounded in 1997, we are a professional enterprise specialized in the design, production and international trade of horticultural flower baskets.

For 30 years, we have always adhered to the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Foremost", and focused on providing global customers with high-quality horticultural flower basket products and professional international trade services.

The company boasts a modern production base and a professional design team, with its products exported to numerous countries and regions across Europe and North America.

All our products comply with the EU REACH environmental standards, providing customers with safe and reliable product guarantees.
